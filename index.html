Sede

If you enjoy reading poetry, I have written some pieces that I would like to share with you.

Stranger
Thoughts wild up inside my head
Hiding my emotions deep beneath
Pretending I've forgotten'bout you.

Our conversation buried in the past;
My feelings for you linger within me,
We still stumble upon each other;
But now we are nothing more then a stranger.
Envision
Met without a sight
Departed like the monsoon rain
The moments of our time
Are now just memories

Unsure to meet again
But in my heart l'll always remember
That we reside under the same sky
And stare at the same moon.
Death's-head
Lost of consciousness
In our past, reminiscing

Trying to lose sight of
However it's futile

Yet again recouping
Undying thoughts

Wishing to abandoned
Nevertheless none to keep it alive.
Melancholy
The sky is turning grey
The clouds are thundering

l'll be inside my room
Imagining you next to me

The sky will start sheding
And there will be no sunshine

l'll just slip under blanket
Hoping to meet you in my dream.
